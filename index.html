How it works

LunchBox WORKS IN FOUR SIMPLE STEPS

1-SEARCH

It's simple, on the LunchBox homepage, enter the name of the restaurant you are looking for and click on search. Alternatively, click on the search button on the top menu to view the list of all restaurants.

2-LOCATE

Get to where the restaurant s located by clicking on the restaurant profile. It's even more exciting, you can get the location from anywhere in the country.

3-BOOK A TABLE

Click on the book table button to make a reservation in the restaurant of your choice. We also follow you booking to make sure it get response on time.

4-ENJOY

Once you've placed your reservation, relax and wait. You will get response in the next few minutes and get ready to join the restaurant and have your meal. You can also visit our blog to see the latest restaurants trends in the country.
